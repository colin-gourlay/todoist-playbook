# Daily Review

A GTD-aligned daily process to stay on top of commitments, protect deep work time, and close out each day with clarity.

This template is designed to be used twice per day: once in the morning to plan, and once in the evening to close out.

---

## Objective

- Clear all inboxes and capture new inputs
- Confirm today's priorities before diving in
- Match work to energy levels
- Close open loops at end of day

Estimated duration: 10–15 minutes.

---

## When to Use

- Morning (start of workday)
- Evening (end of workday)
- Whenever feeling scattered or overwhelmed mid-day

---

## Structure Overview

1. Capture & Clarify
2. Review Today's Commitments
3. Context & Energy Check
4. End-of-Day Close

---

## GTD Alignment

This template follows core GTD principles:

- **Capture**: Every input is collected and processed
- **Clarify**: Each item is actioned (Do / Delegate / Defer / Delete)
- **Organise**: Tasks are placed in the right context and prioritised
- **Reflect**: Daily review of today's commitments and waiting-for items
- **Engage**: Selecting the right work based on context, time, and energy

---

## Suggested Ritual

- Morning: run sections 1–3 before starting any focused work
- Evening: run section 4 before logging off
- Silence notifications during the review
- Keep the review under 15 minutes

---

## Import Instructions

1. Download `template.csv`
2. Create a new project in Todoist
3. Import from CSV
4. Rename project to: `Daily Review – [DD/MM]`
5. Repeat daily or use as a recurring project
