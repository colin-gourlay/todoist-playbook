# Radio Show Promotion

A workflow for promoting a radio show across social channels — both before and after the broadcast.

This template handles the full promotion lifecycle, from pre-show announcements through to post-show engagement.

---

## Objective

- Announce the show across relevant social platforms before broadcast
- Share the show playlist preview to build anticipation
- Publish post-show content and engage with the audience

---

## When to Use

- In the days before and after every broadcast
- As part of the Radio Show Week Kit bundle
- When launching a new show or growing an existing audience

---

## Structure Overview

1. Pre-Show Promotion
2. Post-Show Promotion

---

## Import Instructions

1. Download `template.csv`
2. Create a new project in Todoist
3. Import from CSV
4. Rename the project to: `Radio Show Promotion – Week of [date]`
5. Complete pre-show tasks before broadcast and post-show tasks within 24 hours of going live
