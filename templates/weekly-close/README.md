# Weekly Close

A Friday shutdown ritual to close open loops before the weekend.

This template is the end-of-week half of the two-part weekly system:

- [Weekly Close](../weekly-close/) for Friday reflection and closure
- [Weekly Plan](../weekly-plan/) for Sunday planning and prioritization

---

## Objective

- Close unfinished loops from the week
- Process inboxes and loose capture points
- Review commitments and performance signals
- Carry forward only intentional priorities

Estimated duration: 45 minutes.

---

## When to Use

- Friday afternoon or early evening
- At the end of your final work block of the week

---

## Structure Overview

1. Close the Past
2. Clear the Present
3. Review Commitments
4. Performance Review & Alignment
5. Stop / Start / Continue

---

## Why This Works

Weekly close is intentionally backward-looking. It follows GTD-style review habits:

- Collect and process open loops
- Reconcile commitments and waiting-fors
- Capture lessons while they are still fresh

By finishing this pass on Friday, Sunday planning becomes lighter and cleaner.

---

## Import Instructions

1. Download `template.csv`
2. Create a new project in Todoist
3. Import from CSV
4. Rename project to: `Weekly Close - [DD/MM]`
5. Complete before ending your workweek
