# House Admin

An annual household administration checklist covering bills, renewals, vehicle maintenance, and home upkeep.

---

## Objective

- Keep household finances optimised and under control
- Ensure all insurance policies are current and competitive
- Maintain the vehicle and avoid MOT surprises
- Stay on top of property maintenance before issues escalate
- Keep important documents organised and backed up

Estimated duration: 2 hours (once or twice per year).

---

## When to Use

- Annually as a household audit
- When approaching insurance or contract renewal dates
- When a vehicle MOT or service is due
- When reviewing household spending

---

## Structure Overview

1. Bills & Utilities
2. Insurance Renewals
3. Vehicle & MOT
4. Property & Home Maintenance
5. Finance & Records

---

## Suggested Ritual

- Run this checklist once in January and once in July
- Block a half-day to work through it without interruption
- Have your bank statements, insurance documents, and MOT history available
- Use a password manager to store any updated login credentials

---

## Import Instructions

1. Download `template.csv`
2. Create a new project in Todoist
3. Import from CSV
4. Rename project to: `House Admin – [Year]`
5. Work through sections at your own pace — prioritise items with approaching deadlines
