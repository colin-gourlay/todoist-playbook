# Artist Interview Invite Workflow

A practical checklist for inviting artists or bands into the East Coast FM studio in Haddington for a live interview, then handling follow-up, booking, and preparation.

---

## Objective

- Identify suitable artists and prioritize outreach
- Send concise, high-impact invite emails
- Follow up professionally to maximize response rates
- Confirm logistics and prepare a strong studio interview

---

## When to Use

- When you want to invite a musician or band for a live studio interview
- When contacting an artist directly or via management, label, or agent
- When running repeated outreach cycles for future shows

---

## Structure Overview

1. Target Artists & Research
2. Contact Discovery
3. Initial Outreach Email
4. Follow-Up Cadence
5. Booking & Studio Logistics
6. Interview Preparation
7. Post-Confirmation Admin

---

## Copy/Paste Email Templates

Use the templates below when you reach the email tasks in the checklist.

### Initial Invite Email

Subject options:

1. Live Studio Interview Invite: East Coast FM (Tuesday 8-10pm)
2. Interview Invitation for [Artist Name] - East Coast FM, Haddington
3. Feature [Artist Name] on Sundown Sessions (East Coast FM)

Email body:

Hi [Name/Team],

I am Colin, presenter of Sundown Sessions on East Coast FM (Haddington), every Tuesday from 8:00 to 10:00pm.

I am a genuine fan of [Artist/Band Name] and have regularly featured your music on the show. With your new release, I would love to invite [Artist Name] into our studio for a live interview to talk about the new record, your journey so far, and what is next.

If helpful, here are links to the show:
- East Coast FM: www.eastcoastfm.co.uk
- Sundown Sessions Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/sundownsessionsuk
- Show website: https://sundownsessions.co.uk/
- Recent episode (Mixcloud): https://www.mixcloud.com/sundownsessions/sundown-sessions-show-34-feat-kelowna-apology-none-television-compulsive-gamblers-the-move/

If this is of interest, I would be delighted to arrange a date that suits your schedule.

Best regards,
Colin
Sundown Sessions, East Coast FM
