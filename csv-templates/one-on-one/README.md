# One-on-One

A recurring preparation and follow-up checklist for 1:1 meetings with a manager or direct report.

---

## Objective

- Arrive at each 1:1 prepared with clear talking points
- Ensure agreed actions are captured and followed up
- Maintain a continuous thread across sessions
- Use 1:1s for both operational alignment and career development

Estimated duration: 30 minutes (including 10 minutes of preparation).

---

## When to Use

- Before any scheduled 1:1 with your manager
- Before any 1:1 with a direct report (if you are a manager)
- As a recurring weekly or fortnightly ritual

---

## Structure Overview

1. Before the Meeting
2. During the Meeting
3. After the Meeting

---

## Suggested Ritual

- Review notes from the previous session the day before
- Keep a running list of topics between sessions
- Complete the "After the Meeting" section within 24 hours
- Do not let actions from the 1:1 fall into the general backlog without a due date

---

## Import Instructions

1. Download `template.csv`
2. Create a new project in Todoist
3. Import from CSV
4. Rename project to: `1:1 – [Name] – [Date]`
5. Repeat for each session, or use as a recurring project template
