# Socials Health & Optimisation Checklist

A periodic audit to maintain brand hygiene, consistency, and security across all public-facing platforms — social networks, professional directories, review sites, creative portfolios, and any other space where you present yourself publicly.

This template is not limited to social media. It covers any platform where your brand, identity, or reputation is visible: LinkedIn, Facebook, X (Twitter), Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, GitHub, Behance, Medium, Substack, Google Business Profile, Trustpilot, and beyond.

---

## Objective

- Ensure your profile information is accurate, current, and consistent across all platforms
- Maintain a coherent visual identity and brand voice
- Keep your content strategy aligned with your audience and goals
- Monitor and protect your online reputation
- Maintain strong security hygiene across every account
- Ensure legal compliance and content accessibility

Estimated duration: 90–120 minutes.

---

## When to Use

- Every 4 weeks as a recurring brand maintenance ritual
- After a rebrand, name change, or significant career/business pivot
- After a period of inactivity on any platform
- Before launching a new campaign, product, or public initiative
- Whenever you feel your online presence feels inconsistent or outdated

## Security Best Practices

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on every platform
- Never reuse passwords — generate and store via a password manager
- Regularly audit third-party app access and revoke anything unused
- Keep account recovery contacts (email and phone) up to date
- Act immediately on any suspicious login alerts
